Cherating Beach

Experience the laid-back charm of Cherating Beach, known for its golden sands and excellent surfing conditions. Enjoy water sports, visit a turtle sanctuary, or simply relax in a hammock under swaying palm trees.

Fraser's Hill

Escape to the cool highlands of Fraser's Hill, a quaint colonial-era retreat. Enjoy nature walks, birdwatching, and golf amidst lush greenery and misty mountains.
